Responsible Gaming

Dos & Don'ts of Responsible Gaming

Dos

  • Set time limits for gaming to balance with homework, hobbies, and sleep.
  • Take regular breaks to rest your eyes and stretch your body.
  • Choose age-appropriate games with positive content.
  • Play with friends or family to make it social and fun.
  • Use gaming as a reward after completing tasks or chores.

Don'ts

  • Don’t skip meals, schoolwork, or sleep to play games.
  • Don’t play games that encourage violence or harmful behavior.
  • Don’t spend too much money on in-game purchases without permission.
  • Don’t let gaming interfere with friendships or family time.
  • Don’t ignore physical health — stay active outside the game.
