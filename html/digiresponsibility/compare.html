Cyber Security vs Digital Responsibility

Comparison of Cybersecurity and Digital Responsibility
Cybersecurity Digital Responsibility
Technology, tools and rules that keep devices, accounts and data safe from hackers. Good behaviour and choices online that respect others and keep everyone safe.
Protects systems, passwords, networks and personal data from attacks. Focuses on privacy, kindness, and how our actions affect others.
Goal: Prevent breaches, theft and damage to digital systems. Goal: Create a safe, fair and respectful online community.
Examples:
  • Using strong passwords
  • Installing antivirus software
  • Enabling multi-factor authentication
 Examples:
  • Asking permission before posting a friend’s photo
  • Not sharing secrets online
  • Reporting cyberbullying
Tips:
  • Use different strong passwords for each account.
  • Turn on updates and security settings.
 Tips:
  • Think before you post — be kind.
  • Ask permission before sharing info about others.
Cybersecurity = strong locks to protect your digital world. Digital Responsibility = good manners to make the digital world better for everyone.
