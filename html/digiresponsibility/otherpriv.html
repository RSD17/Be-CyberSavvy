Respecting Others' Privacy

Dos & Don'ts of Respecting Others' Privacy

Dos:

  • Always ask before sharing someone’s photo, video, or personal details.
  • Use privacy settings to control who can see shared content.
  • Keep private conversations within the group and don’t leak screenshots.
  • Respect secrets when someone trusts you with personal information.
  • Model good behavior by valuing and protecting others’ privacy.

Don'ts:

  • Don’t post or share others’ addresses, phone numbers, or sensitive details online.
  • Don’t tag people in photos or posts without their permission.
  • Don’t forward private messages, emails, or screenshots without consent.
  • Don’t try to spy on or access accounts, devices, or files that aren’t yours.
  • Don’t pressure others to share personal information they wish to keep private.
