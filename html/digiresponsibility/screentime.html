Responsible Screentime Management

Why is it important to manage your screen time?

Managing your screen time is important because spending too much time on phones, computers, or games can affect your health, focus, and relationships. Too much screen use can cause eye strain, poor sleep, and less time for physical activities or face-to-face interactions. By balancing screen time with homework, exercise, hobbies, and rest, you can stay healthier, improve concentration, and enjoy both the digital and real world in a positive way.
