Strong Passwords

Why do you need strong passwords?

  • Helps maintain your online privacy
  • Prevents hackers from stealing your personal information
  • Protects your accounts from unauthorized access
  • Reduces the risk of identity theft
  • Ensures the security of your sensitive data
  • Safeguards your financial information

How you can create a strong password:

  1. Use at least 8 characters
  2. Include a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
  3. Include numbers
  4. Add special characters (e.g., !@#$%^&*)
  5. Avoid using personal information (Eg: Birthdays, names, schools etc.)

Dos & Don'ts

Dos:

  • Use a password manager to store your passwords securely
  • Change your passwords regularly
  • Enable two-factor authentication where possible
  • Use unique passwords for different accounts
  • Always keep your passwords fully private

Don'ts:

  • Don't share your passwords with anyone
  • Don't use the same password across multiple sites
  • Don't write down your passwords in easily accessible places
  • Don't use obvious passwords (Eg: "password123", "yourname@123")
  • Don't ignore password security warnings
